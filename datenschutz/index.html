What We Collect

This website collects no personal data beyond standard server logs (IP address, browser type, timestamp) which are stored automatically by the hosting provider and deleted on a rolling basis. No tracking, no analytics, no advertising cookies.

Contact Form

When you send a message via the contact form, your name, email address and message content are used solely to respond to your enquiry. Data is not shared with third parties and is deleted once no longer needed.

Newsletter / Updates

If you subscribe to project updates via the footer form, only your email address is collected. It is used exclusively to send design and project news. You can unsubscribe at any time by contacting nicole@kidashidesign.com. No email marketing platform or third-party provider is used at this time — emails are sent directly.

Hosting & Server Logs

This website is hosted by Hostinger International Ltd. The hosting provider automatically stores access logs (IP address, browser type, timestamp, pages visited) for security and operation purposes. These logs are not accessible to us and are deleted on a rolling basis by Hostinger. See Hostinger's Privacy Policy for details.

Your Rights (GDPR Art. 15–22)

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing of, and port any personal data held about you. You may also object to processing and withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, contact: nicole@kidashidesign.com

If you are located in the EU/EEA, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with the data protection supervisory authority in your country of residence.
