Beyond Design

Mixed Media Art, Shaped by Travel.

Alongside my design work, I create mixed media art collages — process-driven pieces shaped by the places I've lived and traveled through, from Tbilisi to Bangkok to the Himalayas. Each piece blends photography, drawing, and found materials into something that captures a feeling more than a literal place.
