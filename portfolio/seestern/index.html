Seestern Britzer Garten

2022

Where Nature Meets Brand

Full brand identity for a lakeside venue in Berlin's Britzer Garten park. A playful seahorse logo with a nature-inspired palette — extended to print and merchandise that guests actually want to keep.

CategoryBrand Identity
Year2022
ScopeLogo with seahorse variations, t-shirts & merchandise, menu card, business cards

Process & Approach

01

Logo & Character

Designed a seahorse logo with multiple variations — a versatile mark that feels equally at home on signage, t-shirts, and menu covers.

02

Palette & Feel

Built a nature-inspired color palette rooted in the teal tones of the lake and surrounding park, evoking freshness and calm.

03

Print & Merch

Extended the identity across menu cards, business cards, and branded merchandise — ensuring every guest touchpoint reinforces the brand story.
