Art Gerecht Modular

Category Print Design · Branding
Year 2024
Client Art Gerecht
Services Print Design, Modular Identity
Print Design Brand Identity Modular System

A flyer that becomes a system.

Art Gerecht needed a print identity that could adapt to every event without losing its character. The result is a modular flyer system — a single format that rotates through editions, each with a distinct front, but bound together by consistent structure, typography, and an earthy material palette drawn from concrete, paper, and gold.

“The same piece, reimagined every time — that's what makes it memorable.”

— Nicole Szatkowski, Kidashi Design

Process & Approach

3 Steps
01

System Design

A modular grid developed to hold any visual content while keeping brand recognition across editions — logo, hierarchy, and material language locked in place.

02

Material Palette

Concrete texture, uncoated paper tones, and a warm gold — materials chosen to evoke the raw, tactile quality of independent art spaces.

03

Edition Rollout

Front and back faces designed as a double-sided print — each edition tells its own story while the reverse anchors the recurring identity.
