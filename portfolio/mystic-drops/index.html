Packaging Print

Two packaging personalities — one brand world.

Energy tablet brand requiring two distinct packaging versions — one targeting women, one targeting men — while maintaining a unified brand language across both.

“Different audiences, same brand confidence.”

— Nicole Szatkowski, Kidashi Design

Process & Approach

3 Steps
01

Concept Development

Defined two distinct visual languages — energetic/bold for men, refined/empowering for women — within a single brand system.

02

Label Design

Full label artwork for each version — front, back and side panels designed for shelf impact.

03

Print Production

Files prepared to industry packaging standards with die line, bleed, CMYK profiles and supplier handoff documentation.
