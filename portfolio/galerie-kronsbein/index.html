Galerie Kronsbein

2022

Timeless, Refined, Renewed

Logo redesign and visual identity refresh for an established art gallery. Refined typography and a palette that respects the gallery's heritage while bringing it into the present.

CategoryBrand Identity
Year2022
ScopeLogo redesign, color palette, typography system

Process & Approach

01

Heritage Research

Studied the gallery's history and existing visual assets to understand what needed to evolve and what had to stay — respect for the past is the foundation of a good redesign.

02

Logo Redesign

Created a refined logomark that signals quality and permanence, with letterforms drawn from classical proportions adapted for contemporary application.

03

Identity System

Defined a complete color palette and typography system that works across print collateral, digital channels, and in-gallery signage.
