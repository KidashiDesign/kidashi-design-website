XP-Days

Category Web Design · Brand Identity · Social Media
Year 2022–2023
Client Esports Event Organizer
Services Logo, Web Design, Social Media
Web Design Brand Identity Social Media

A complete esports brand — from first logo to live tournament platform.

Freelance collaboration with an esports event organizer. Full creative scope from logo to digital touchpoints — building a cohesive visual world across every platform the brand touches: event posters, the web presence, giveaway campaigns, and an ongoing social media identity.

What We Delivered

3 Areas
01

Brand Foundation

Logo design and complete corporate design guidelines establishing the XP-Days visual identity — color system, typography hierarchy, icon set, and a comprehensive rulebook covering every touchpoint from merchandise to full-bleed digital formats.

02

Digital Design

Homepage and subpage banners optimised for desktop and mobile, plus a complete tournament platform UI built in Figma — covering registration flows, bracket displays, the Aussteller section, and the giveaway landing experience.

03

Social & Campaign

A full content calendar with recurring post templates, giveaway graphics, and event-countdown assets — followed by hands-on management of the brand's social media presence throughout the event season and beyond.

“Every touchpoint of the brand felt intentional and competitive.”

— Nicole Szatkowski, Kidashi Design
