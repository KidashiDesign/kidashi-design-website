About Me

The Designer Behind Kidashi Design

Hi, I'm Nicole. I'm a certified graphic designer with a background that's a little less linear than most — and that's exactly what shapes how I work with clients today.

Let's Work Together

My Path

I completed my IHK-certified training as a Media Designer (Digital & Print) at the Mediadesign University of Applied Sciences (MD.H) in Munich. Before that, I spent three years in content management at Ajaska GmbH, building and growing social media channels for several digital magazines.

That experience still shapes how I think about design today: it's not just about how something looks, it's about what it communicates and who it's for. After my training, I worked as a graphic designer for several agencies, gaining hands-on experience across web design, branding, print, and social media, before going freelance in 2025 under my own studio, Kidashi Design.

Where I Work From

I'm currently based in Tbilisi, Georgia, working as a digital nomad — which means I bring a flexible, remote-friendly way of working to every project, while staying easy to reach and personally involved from start to finish, no matter where you're based.

How I Work

I work well independently, but the best ideas usually come from working closely with my clients, not around them. I bring structure and fast turnarounds, paired with genuine curiosity about your business — so the result doesn't just look good, it actually fits.
